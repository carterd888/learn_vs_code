What did i learn today?

today i decided to check my website and make it more accessable

i have already been using figcaption and alt text with my latest blog pages, however i decided to review and update earlier pages

the next thing i looked at was the contrast of my site

using the inspect tool on google chrome i was able to see how the contrast was rated

to improve this i updated the font-size for paragraphs to 24

i also decided that for other aspects of text such as figcaption and quotes i would use a better contrast of colours so i could keep my font size smaller

as a result of increasing the text size i noticed it occasionaly went off screen on the right side

to counter this i set a width of 90% for the paragraphs
